Know Our Food Campaign

This is part of the McDonald’s ‘Know Our Food’ campaign, which sought to debunk misconceptions around the restaurant’s food and products. We wanted to educate consumers on what goes into their favourite meals in an eye-catching and unique way that would not only resonate with the public, but would dispel common myths surrounding the brand’s products too.
