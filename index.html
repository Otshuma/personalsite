Osmond Tshuma - Graphic Designer & Typographer

I’m a Zimbabwean designer, art director, and typographer based in New York, USA. I specialize in crafting thoughtful, story-driven identities and design systems that merge research, symbolism, and strong visual form. With over a decade of experience leading creative projects across cultural, non-profit, and commercial sectors, I bring a distinctive sensitivity to visual language; balancing clarity, emotional resonance, and narrative intent across brand, editorial, and type design.
